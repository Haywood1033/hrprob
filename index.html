⚾ HRProb — How to Use
MLB Home Run Probability Engine · Data refreshes automatically
What This Tool Does
HRProb calculates each batter's probability of hitting a home run today using real Statcast data, pitcher matchup quality, park factors, weather, platoon splits, and recent form. It then compares that probability against DraftKings book odds to identify +EV betting opportunities.
The Tabs

Landing page — daily digest showing Best Play, Top HR Targets, Top Signal Plays, Hidden Edge, and Best Environments. Start here every day.
☰ Players

⬡ Games

🎯 Top Targets
Top 24 unique players by HR probability with signal panels and composite badges. Click any card to expand.
🎰 Parlays
Best 2, 3, and 4-leg HR parlays built from today's top candidates.

Model calibration — actual HR rate vs predicted rate by probability tier. Updates automatically each morning.
Reading the +EV Badge
+EV +4.2%
Our model gives this player a higher HR probability than the book's implied odds. Positive expected value — the model likes this play.
FAIR
Model and book are within 2% of each other. No meaningful edge either way.
-EV
Book implies a higher probability than our model. The price is too short — pass on this one.
💡 +EV doesn't mean it will hit. It means the odds are in your favor over a large sample. Always manage bet sizing accordingly.
Confidence Tier Badge
Every player carries a confidence badge based on their 2026 plate appearance sample. The HR% shown is a point estimate — the badge tells you how much to trust it.
🔒 HIGH
200+ PA. Statcast numbers are statistically stable. Trust the HR% — uncertainty band is roughly ±1.5-2%.
⚡ MID
75-199 PA. Directionally reliable but still some noise. The number is probably in the right ballpark. Uncertainty band ±2.5-3.5%.
⚠ LOW
Under 75 PA. Small sample — Bayesian regression pulls stats toward league average. Use as a directional signal only. Uncertainty band ±4-5%+.
💡 When two players have similar HR% but different confidence tiers, lean toward the HIGH confidence player for betting purposes. A LOW player at 14% could realistically be anywhere from 9-19%.
Other Player Badges
K 32%
High strikeout rate (28%+). Doesn't affect HR probability — purely informational. A player hitting .280 with 32% K rate is a different risk profile than one at 15% K rate, even at the same HR%.
🔥🔥 Scorching
3+ HR in last 20 plate appearances. Model applies a +15% boost to their HR probability.
🔥 Hot
1-2 HR in last 20 PA. Model applies a +7% boost.
❄️ Cold
0 HR and low SLG in last 20 PA. Model applies a -10% penalty.
Reading the Expand Row
Click any player row or card to expand the full breakdown:
HR%
Our model's probability for today's specific matchup — not a season average. Includes park, weather, pitcher, platoon, and streak adjustments.
ISO
Isolated Power — the purest measure of raw power. Higher = more HR upside.
Barrel%
Percentage of batted balls that are barreled (optimal launch angle + exit velocity). Strongest single predictor of home runs.
Last 20 PA
HR count and AVG over the last 20 plate appearances. 🔥🔥 = scorching, 🔥 = hot, ❄️ = cold.
Platoon
Real MLB Stats API splits — actual ISO and SLG vs today's pitcher handedness. Also shows pitcher's FIP vs this batter's hand specifically (red = pitcher is weaker vs this hand).
H2H
Career stats against today's specific pitcher. 2 HR in 15 AB against this guy is a meaningful signal.
Signal Indicator Panel
Every player shows a 5-icon panel. Active signals are full brightness — dimmed means that factor isn't firing today.
🏟
Park — hitter-friendly park (PF ≥1.05) or career SLG boost at this venue (≥20% above season average).
💨
Weather — wind blowing out ≥8mph at an open-air park. Ball carries further, HR rate increases.
🎯
Pitcher matchup — pitcher is vulnerable (xERA ≥4.80 or HR/9 ≥1.40). Also factors in pitcher's FIP vs this batter's handedness specifically.
🔥
Hot streak — player has hit at least 1 HR in their last 20 plate appearances. 🔥🔥 = scorching (3+ HR).
🔒
Confidence — MID (75-199 PA) or HIGH (200+ PA) sample size. Enough data to trust the probability number.
🏏
Pull Air% Edge — batter pull% ≥40% facing a fly ball pitcher (GB% ≤38%). Pulled fly balls against pitchers who give them up — the specific batted ball edge.

Composite Play Badges
Awarded when multiple signals converge on a player. Completely independent of probability — a 10% player with 4 signals gets the same badge as a 20% player with 4 signals.
🏆 Elite Play
5 or 6 signals firing. Rare alignment — the model is converging from every angle including batted ball profile.
⭐ All-Star Play
4 of 6 signals active. Strong multi-factor conviction. These are the plays to prioritize regardless of raw probability ranking.
💡 Value Play
3 of 6 signals active. Multiple reasons to like this player — worth a closer look even if probability is modest.
💡 The ⚡ Today tab shows the Best Play of the Day — a single card combining signal count, streak heat, park history, confidence, and probability into one composite score.
⚡ Today Tab
Best Play Today
Single card showing the highest composite score player — weighted by signal count, streak strength, career park boost, confidence, and probability. This is the model's top recommendation for the day.
Top HR Targets
Top 5 by raw HR probability. The players with the statistically highest chance of homering today.
Top Signal Plays
Top 5 ranked by signal convergence — Elite Play first, then All-Star, then Value. A player can rank higher here than in Top Targets if they have more signals firing despite a lower raw probability. Click "See all →" to view all signal plays in the Players tab.
Hidden Edge
Mid-probability players (6-15%) with at least one strong signal. The fringe plays the market may be overlooking.
Best Environments
Top 6 games ranked by park factor + wind conditions. Click any card to open the game modal.
Games Tab — Click to Expand
Click any game card to open a full overlay showing both lineups sorted by batting order, with HR%, confidence tier, +EV, K rate flag, and streak for every player. Also shows pitcher xERA, HR/9, Whiff%, and projected strikeout total for both starters.
🔮 Forecast Tab
Expected HRs
Sum of all individual HR probabilities across the slate — the statistical expectation for total home runs today. Includes a 90% confidence interval range.
Hot Batters
Expandable section showing 🔥🔥 Scorching and 🔥 Hot players with their current form stats and book odds.
Pitcher K Props
Projected strikeout totals for every starter based on their Whiff% (K% ≈ Whiff% × 0.68 + 3.5) and expected innings (based on xERA quality). Green = 7K+, teal = 5-6K.
First Inning Scoring
Probability that either team scores in the 1st inning. Uses real 1st-inning pitcher splits where available, falling back to overall xERA proxy. Base rate is 26% per team per inning.
🔒 NRFI Target
No Run First Inning — games where the model projects ≤38% probability of scoring. Both pitchers are projecting clean 1st innings.
🔥 YRFI Target
Yes Run First Inning — games at ≥55% probability. High likelihood at least one team scores in the 1st.
Daily Workflow
1. Open the app — schedule, lineups, weather and Statcast load automatically
2. If the yellow banner appears, click ✦ Odds to refresh DraftKings HR props and O/U totals
3. Wait ~30 seconds for hot streak and platoon split data to load
4. Check Top Targets for the day's best opportunities
5. Use Parlays tab for multi-leg combinations
6. Check Forecast tab for K props, NRFI/YRFI targets, and HR total projection
7. Confirmed lineups replace projections automatically as managers post them (2-3 hrs before first pitch)
💡 The ~ badge means projected lineup. The ✓ badge means confirmed batting order. Always wait for confirmed lineups before placing bets.
